Getting Started

Everything you need to know to get started with the LearnTech platform.

How do I create an account on LearnTech? ▼

Creating an account on LearnTech is easy! Simply follow these steps:

  1. Go to the LearnTech homepage and click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  2. Enter your email address, create a password, and fill in your personal information
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  4. Complete your profile information and preferences
  5. Start exploring courses and learning resources!

If you're signing up through your school or organization, you may need to use a specific registration link or code provided by your administrator.

How do I navigate the LearnTech dashboard? ▼

The LearnTech dashboard is your central hub for all platform activities. Here's a quick overview:

  • Left Sidebar: Navigate between different sections of the platform
  • Main Dashboard: Shows your active courses, upcoming assignments, and recent activities
  • Course Cards: Click on any course card to access course materials
  • Calendar Widget: View upcoming deadlines and scheduled events
  • Notifications: Check for important announcements and updates

You can customize your dashboard view in the account settings to show the information most relevant to you.

How do I enroll in a course? ▼

To enroll in a course, follow these steps:

  1. From your dashboard, click on "Browse Courses" or go to the Courses tab
  2. Browse available courses or use the search function to find specific topics
  3. Click on a course to view details, curriculum, and instructor information
  4. Click the "Enroll" button to join the course

Depending on your subscription plan:

  • Some courses may require additional payment
  • Some courses may require an enrollment key (provided by your instructor)
  • If you're a student, your courses might be pre-assigned based on your program

Once enrolled, the course will appear on your dashboard and in your "My Courses" section.

How do I reset my password? ▼

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

  1. On the login page, click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam/junk folders if you don't see it)
  4. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password
  5. Use your new password to log in

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link has expired, simply restart the process.

If you continue to have issues, please contact our support team for assistance.

What are the system requirements for using LearnTech? ▼

LearnTech is a web-based platform that works on most modern devices. Here are the minimum requirements:

For computers (Windows, Mac, Linux):

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - latest version or one version prior)
  • Minimum 4GB RAM (8GB recommended)
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps download speed)
  • Speakers or headphones for audio content
  • Webcam and microphone for interactive sessions (optional)

For mobile devices:

  • iOS 13+ or Android 9+
  • LearnTech mobile app (recommended) or mobile web browser
  • Stable internet connection

For the best experience, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer, especially for assignments requiring extensive typing or specialized software.

Courses

Information about accessing and managing your courses.

How do I access my course materials? ▼

To access your course materials:

  1. Log in to your LearnTech account
  2. Navigate to "My Courses" from the sidebar
  3. Select the course you want to access
  4. Browse through different sections of the course using the course navigation menu

Course materials typically include:

  • Video lectures and presentations
  • Reading materials and PDF documents
  • Interactive exercises and quizzes
  • Discussion forums
  • Assignments and projects

Most materials can be accessed online, and many can also be downloaded for offline use.

Can I access courses on mobile devices? ▼

Yes, LearnTech courses are accessible on mobile devices in two ways:

1. LearnTech Mobile App (Recommended)

  • Download from Apple App Store or Google Play Store
  • Log in with your LearnTech credentials
  • Access all your courses and materials
  • Receive notifications for assignments and announcements
  • Download content for offline viewing

2. Mobile Web Browser

  • Visit the LearnTech website on your mobile browser
  • Log in to your account
  • Access your courses through the responsive web interface

While most course content is mobile-friendly, some interactive elements or specialized software might work best on desktop/laptop computers.

How do I track my progress in a course? ▼

LearnTech provides several ways to track your course progress:

Within each course:

  • Progress Bar: Shows overall completion percentage for the course
  • Module Checkmarks: Indicate completed lectures, readings, and activities
  • Grade Center: Displays scores for quizzes, assignments, and assessments

On your dashboard:

  • Course Cards: Display completion percentage for each enrolled course
  • Activity Timeline: Shows recent course activities and achievements
  • Learning Analytics: Provides insights on your study habits and performance

You can also download a progress report from your profile settings if you need documentation of your learning activities.
